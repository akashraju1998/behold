How It Works

Get mailed free products

We'll mail you the products that brands send us.

Place items in your car

Place the products in the back of your car. We send you a guide on how to do this.

Get paid

Everytime someone tries the product, you get paid.

Getting items

Brands send all sorts of items ranging from consumer electronics to beauty products. We ship them to you in a box with instructions on how to place them in a car. When we want to send you a new set of products, all you have to do is sent the box back with the old items using our free return label!

How it works in your car

We send you a clear guide on how to place the products in your car! Essentially, we use the back of the front seats to place the products in an easy manner for riders to use the products only if they want to. Once you place the products, you don't have to do anything until you return the products.

Getting Paid

You get paid each time a rider uses the product. In addition, we provide an easy way for riders to purchase the product online. If they purchase it in your car, you get a percent of sales. There is no cap on how much you can make in a month.You get paid weekly.
